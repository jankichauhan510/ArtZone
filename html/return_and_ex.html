Return and Exchange



At ArtZone, we are committed to supporting small-scale businesses and ensuring customer satisfaction. We understand that sometimes, returns and exchanges may be necessary. This policy outlines the procedures for returning or exchanging products purchased from our platform.

Eligibility for Returns and Exchanges:

You may be eligible for a return or exchange if you have purchased a product through ArtZone. Eligibility criteria may vary depending on the policies of the individual seller, so please review the specific seller's return and exchange policy before making a purchase.

Timeframe for Returns and Exchanges:

The timeframe for initiating a return or exchange may vary from seller to seller. Please check the seller's product listing or contact the seller directly for information on their specific return and exchange timeframe.

Return and Exchange Process:

To initiate a return or exchange, follow these general steps:
Contact the seller: Reach out to the seller through ArtZone and inform them of your intention to return or exchange the product.
Follow the seller's instructions: The seller will provide you with instructions on how to proceed, which may include returning the item to a specified address or providing additional information.
Await seller approval: The seller will assess your request and approve it if it meets their return and exchange policy criteria.
Return the product: If approved, follow the seller's instructions for returning the product. Ensure it is in the condition specified by the seller's policy.

Shipping and Handling:

The responsibility for return shipping costs and handling fees may vary depending on the seller's policy. Be sure to review the seller's specific policy for details on who covers these costs.

Refund or Exchange Options:

Sellers may offer refunds, store credits, or exchanges as per their individual policies. Be sure to clarify with the seller which option is available to you.

Timeframe for Processing:

The time it takes to process a return or exchange will vary by seller. Please refer to the seller's policy or contact them for estimated processing times.

Non-Returnable Items:

Certain items sold on our platform may be non-returnable or non-exchangeable. This could include personalized or customized products, perishable goods, or items sold as final sale. Check the seller's product listing for information on whether a product is returnable or exchangeable.

Contact Information:

If you have questions or need assistance with a return or exchange, please reach out to the seller directly using the contact information provided on their store page. For general inquiries or platform-related questions, you can contact ArtZone customer support at ArtZone03@gmail.com

Additional Terms:

Any additional terms and conditions specific to ArtZone or the individual sellers can be found in the seller's product listings or store policies.

Legal Disclaimers:

This policy is subject to change, and ArtZone reserves the right to update it as needed. Thank you for choosing ArtZone to support small-scale businesses. We aim to provide a seamless shopping experience while fostering growth for our sellers. If you have any questions or concerns about our return and exchange policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to us or the respective seller for assistance.
